Data Analytics

Data analytics provides intelligent visualization of data and enables generation of insights from the data.

The three types of analytics which can enable drilling optimization are

  • Descriptive analytics
  • Prescriptive analytics
  • Predictive analytics

Machine vision technologies are replacing legacy data generation technologies. High resolution digital camera can replace digital sensors. The data generated from video streams can enable robotics and automation of operations, e.g., machine vision technologies can help detect slip status of drill string.
